# TheSpaceShooterGame
A basic but cool space shooter experience, made for single player. Made with Python, using the PyGame module.

Fly through space while avoiding asteroids and shooting down alien spaceships. . Try to destroy as many aliens as possible, let too many escape and you lose.
Your spaceship can move left and right on the screen. Crashing into any object means that one of your lives is lost. You only have 3 lives. If you lose all of them, the game ends. Asteroids cannot be destroyed by bullets, so try to avoid them.
Reach the Goal displayed on the screen to move to the next level. Moving to the next level increases the goal. If you lose, you restart on the same level you lost.
Firing will release two bullets from the spaceship's wings, and also award the player with 1 charge. Charge is displayed on the screen at all times. Reaching 10 Charge points will fire a powerful laser beam from the front of the spaceship, destroying anything in its path, including asteroids and aliens.
After firing 5 times, you will have to wait for the spaceship to reload.

Controls:
Movement keys: Left Arrow Key <-, Right Arrow Key ->.
Fire: SPACE.
